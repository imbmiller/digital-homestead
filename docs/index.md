# Digital Homestead

**Take back control of your digital life.**

Digital Homestead is a collection of self-hosting modules that help you replace streaming services, SaaS subscriptions, and cloud dependencies with software you run on your own hardware.

---

## What is digital homesteading?

The modern internet has drifted toward centralization. Your photos live on Google's servers. Your movies are licensed, not owned. Your budget data flows through a SaaS you don't control.

Digital homesteading is the practice of running your own infrastructure: your media server, your photo archive, your finance tools, your network. It doesn't require an advanced degree or enterprise hardware — a desktop PC or mini PC with 8–16 GB of RAM running Ubuntu is enough to host everything in this repo.

---

## What's in this repo

A set of **modules** — each one a documented, tested installation guide for a specific self-hosted service. Every module includes:

- A human-readable overview
- Step-by-step agent instructions (`AGENT.md`) that your AI agent can execute on your machine
- Model-specific notes when agents of a particular model have encountered known issues
